Welcome to UW-Madison Math Club
===============================

.. meta::
   :description: This is the homepage of UW-Madison Math Club, student organization aims to math students at University of Wisconsin - Madison
   :keywords: mathclub, math club, math club uw, math club UW Madison, UW Madison, University of Wisconsin Madison
   :author: Zeyuan Hu

Math club is a student organization that aims to the all math students at `University of Wisconsin - Madison`__ .
We plan a series of events throughout the semester:
    * :ref:`Math Club Lecture series <lectureseries>` -- talks on *fun* mathematical topic
    * :ref:`Math Club Advising Night <advising>`-- a discussion on the major planning and course selection issues

.. admonition:: join us

   There is no membership fee for our club. To join us, you simply need to subscribe our UMC mailing list:
    
        send an email to `mathclublist-request <mathclub-request@math.wisc.edu>`_ with subject "subscribe" and
        reply to the confirmation email.
