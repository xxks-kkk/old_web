Welcome to UW-Madison Math Club¶

Math club is a student organization that aims to the all math students at University of Wisconsin - Madison . We plan a series of events throughout the semester:

  • Math Club Lecture series – talks on fun mathematical topic
  • Math Club Advising Night– a discussion on the major planning and course selection issues

join us

There is no membership fee for our club. To join us, you simply need to subscribe our UMC mailing list:

send an email to mathclublist-request with subject “subscribe” and reply to the confirmation email.
